MAKING A DIFFERENCE

We’re advocating for the development of a local human breast milk bank in Saskatchewan, and we need your support. Please contact us if you can volunteer or advocate for this growing and valuable service.

Why Donate?

All babies benefit from human breast milk. But for medically fragile or premature infants,
breast milk is even more critical. Even life saving.

That’s where we come in. The Lacta-Care Human Breast Milk Bank is advocates for the
provision of lifesaving breast milk for premature babies, a critical service for families in our
community.

What our Donors and Recipients are saying...

My son's mother was unable to breast feed and NYMB not only made sure that breast milk was readily available for my son, but maintained such a personal relationship with me that they became like family. My son, born one month premature, has been able to grow and thrive so miraculously. NYMB has truly been guardian angels in the lives of my family.

MAKING A DIFFERENCE

At no extra cost to you, you can save a fragile infants life. Donate your excess breast milk to a milk bank. And become the HERO a baby needs.

Why Donate?

Mother’s milk is central to proper development of newborn babies. It protects them from illnesses and makes them healthier, stronger. But some mothers are unable to produce this elixir for their precious ones. That’s where you can help. As a donor of breast milk, even sparing small amounts of your milk can go a long way.

As a mother to a newborn baby, you might produce more milk than your baby needs. A quick call to [Lacto] can provide the lifesaving nourishment a medically fragile baby requires. YOU can be the hero an infant needs! contact us to donate breast milk.
